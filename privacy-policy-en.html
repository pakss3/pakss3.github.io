Privacy Policy (I Like Baby)

The I Like Baby app (hereinafter referred to as "the App") values your privacy and complies with applicable privacy protection laws.

1. Personal Information We Collect

The App collects the following information:

  • Baby Information: Name (nickname), date of birth, gender
  • Childcare Activity Records: Feeding, sleep, diaper changes, temperature, medication, and other related records

2. Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Recording and providing childcare activity statistics
  • Pattern analysis and notification services
  • Service improvement

3. Storage of Personal Information

  • Storage Location: All data is stored only on your device.
  • Server Transmission: The App does not transmit your data to external servers.
  • Retention Period: All data is deleted when the App is uninstalled.

4. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

The App does not provide your personal information to third parties.

5. Advertising and Analytics

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. During this process, Google may collect the following information:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • Ad interaction data

For more information about how Google handles personal information related to advertising, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Destruction of Personal Information

When you uninstall the App, all stored data is automatically deleted.

7. User Rights

You can view, modify, or delete your data at any time through the App's settings.
